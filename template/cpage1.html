Child Gain Chews

The product is aimed at the nutrition and health of children from 1 to 12 years old. It uses freeze-dried fruits and vegetables as raw materials to develop a non-damaging snack. I participated in the research and development and promotion of this original product throughout the entire process, such as the accounting of production costs, material printing, transportation and export costs, and established wholesale prices, retail prices, import and export prices, and so on. According to the characteristics of the product, according to my brand and marketing strategy after redefining, I selected 5 different animals that are most popular with children, carried out creative character design, and based on this, independently designed the visual system and packaging of the product. This includes independent packaging of products, retail store display packaging, and value sets. Communicating and supervising the printing of materials throughout the process, and guided the animated promotional videos of this series of products, which were broadcast at the ciie 2019 exhibition
